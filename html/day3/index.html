A little girl

The theme of the story: The story tells about the girl which facing the problem and how she overcome it.

Lily is a very active schoolgirl. Every day after school, while walking home, she thinks about her future. But in the morning, she forgets those thoughts because she wakes up late, rushes to get ready. This is her daily routine. One day at school, the teacher announced Sports Day. The events included the lemon-and-spoon race, 100-meter race, and more. Lily participated and won second prize in the lemon-and-spoon race. Another girl won two first prizes. Lily did not feel jealous. Instead, she thought, “Next year, I can achieve this too.”

The next year came. After the May holidays, when school reopened, Lily looked for that girl but found she had moved to another school. Still, Lily kept thinking about improving herself. Lily had a close friend named Tamil. They were very good friends. One day during class, Lily felt something new and uncomfortable. The class leader took her to the office room. The teacher called her father. “Hello sir, we are calling to inform you about your daughter.” “What happened, ma’am?” “Don’t worry, it is good news. Please take her home.”

Lily did not understand what was happening, but she noticed people talking around her about the same topic. After that, once every month, she would be sent to the office room and her parents would be called to take her home. The office teacher noticed this and asked her gently. She gave Lily advice and said, “Don’t be afraid. It is a natural part of being a woman.” Those words made Lily realize there was nothing to worry about. Life continued. On the next Sports Day, Lily was rejected from participating because the office teacher said she was not suitable due to missing school during her menstrual days. Lily felt disappointed but did not give up.

She requested her PT teacher for a re-selection, and it was accepted. among 15 students mam said to run with them, in the running race she proved she got selected and felt very happy at that moment. Later, at a government-level competition, Lily represented her school and won. The same teacher who once rejected her saw her victory and smiled with pride.That encouragement motivated Lily to participate in the next event, where she got an championship.
