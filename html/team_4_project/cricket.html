About us

Champion Cricket Academy is dedicated to nurturing young talent and transforming passionate players into professional cricketers. Our academy provides high-quality coaching, modern training facilities, and a disciplined environment to help players improve their skills and confidence. We believe that cricket is not just a game — it builds leadership, teamwork, fitness, and character.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop technically strong, mentally tough, and physically fit cricketers who can compete at district, state, and national levels.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading cricket academies in the country by producing skilled and disciplined players.

Coaching Programs

Beginner Level: Basic batting, bowling, and fielding techniques Introduction to cricket rules Fitness and agility training


Intermediate Level: Advanced batting and bowling practice Match simulations Strength and conditioning sessions

Advanced Level: Professional-level coaching Video analysis sessions Tournament preparation Personality development

Our Facilities

  • International standard practice nets
  • Turf and matting wickets
  • Bowling machines
  • Fitness training area
  • Separate coaching for batting and bowling
  • Weekend practice matches

Why Choose Us?

  • Opportunities to participate in local tournaments
