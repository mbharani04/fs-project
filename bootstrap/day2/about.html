Our Story

Built on the Road,
Driven by People

It began in 2001 with a single petrol bunk on National Highway 45, a modest forecourt, two fuel dispensers, and one unwavering belief — that every driver deserves honest service and quality fuel.

Our founder, Mr. R. Selvakumar, spent over a decade as a transport mechanic before he staked his life savings on that first station. He had seen first-hand how adulterated fuel ruined engines and how indifferent attendants left travellers stranded. He wanted to build something different: a place where trust was the foundation, not an afterthought.

"I did not build a petrol bunk. I built a promise — that when you stop here, you leave better than you arrived."

— R. Selvakumar, Founder

Word spread quickly. Truck drivers radioed ahead about the honest metre readings. Families on weekend drives trusted us for clean restrooms and a safe stop. Local businesses chose us for fleet accounts because our quality never wavered.

Today, Petroxpress operates across 12 locations, serves over 4,000 vehicles daily, and employs more than 180 people — most of them from the communities we serve. We have added EV charging, loyalty programs, and convenience stores, but our soul remains exactly what it was in 2001: reliable, honest, and people-first.
